## Design Philosophy
The app uses a **dark theme** with **gradient accents** to create a modern, professional look suitable for a university hackathon project. The design emphasizes:
- **Clarity**: Easy to understand crowd levels at a glance
- **Visual Appeal**: Beautiful gradients and modern UI elements
- **Functionality**: All information is clearly presented
- **Accessibility**: Color coding with text labels

## Ready for Images
When you add location images, they will automatically replace the gradient placeholders. The design is fully responsive and will look great with real photos!
